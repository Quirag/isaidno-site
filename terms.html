1. What the App is

I Said No is a private journal designed for people working to reduce their people-pleasing pattern — sometimes called the "fawn response." It lets you log moments where you said no, see your own patterns over time, and read short reflections grounded in clinical research on fawn, DBT, IFS, and self-silencing.

The App is a tool for self-reflection and journaling. It is not therapy and it is not a substitute for mental-health care delivered by a qualified clinician. See "Disclaimers" below.
