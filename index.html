What it is

I Said No is a journal. You tap a big NO button when you've said no to your mother, your boss, an ex, a friend, or your partner. You add a note if you want. Over time you see your pattern.

Built around one rule

Your journal lives on your device. There is no account, no cloud sync, no backend. Photos and voice memos stay in your phone's private storage. Voice memos are transcribed on-device — the audio is never written to disk.

What's inside

  • Sovereign Stamp animation with custom haptic when you log a no
  • Action Button shortcut on iPhone 15+ — log without opening the app
  • Calendar prep notifications + Decline Coach 30-second roleplay
  • Permission Slips, Body Cues, Weekly Wrapped
  • A Library of twelve reflections — paraphrasing Walker, Linehan (DBT), IFS, with citations
  • Apple Watch app + complications
  • Therapist PDF export, full data JSON export (GDPR portability)
